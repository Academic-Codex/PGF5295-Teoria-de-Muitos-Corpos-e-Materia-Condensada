Referências Estudos

Referências principais:

  • P. Coleman, Introduction to Many-Body Physics, Cambridge University Press (2015).
  • S. Sachdev, Quantum Phases of Matter, Cambridge University Press (2023).
  • A. L. Fetter e J. D. Walecka, Quantum Theory of Many-Particle Systems, Dover (2003) [ou McGraw-Hill (1971)].
  • E. M. Lifshitz e L. P. Pitaevskii, Statistical Physics, Part 2, Butterworth-Heinemann (1980).
  • X.-G. Wen, Quantum Field Theory of Many-Body Systems, Oxford University Press (2004).
  • Henrik Bruus e Karsten Flensberg, Many-Body Quantum Theory in Condensed Matter Physics, Oxford University Press (2004).

  • Altland e Simons, Condensed Matter Field Theory.
  • Anderson, Basic Notions of Condensed Matter Physics.
  • Blaizot e Ripka, Quantum Theory of Finite Systems.
  • Negele e Orland, Quantum Many-Particle Systems.
